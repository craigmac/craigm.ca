Openroll

Openroll is a free and open-source scoreboard and match timer for IBJJF-rules Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. It is not affiliated with the IBJJF in any way. I learned about:

  • C++ 14 and Qt 5.12.x;
  • cross-platform packaging;
  • QML and QMake build systems;
  • and cross-window communications.
